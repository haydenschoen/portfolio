Designed a paper dinosaur model for children over the age of six. The child constructs the toy by punching die-cut pieces out of one sheet of 1/16in corrugation.

Toy emphasizes shape with a simple color scheme.

Built with a movable jaw that the child can manipulate through an opening in the rear of the head.
